About Us

We believe birthdays deserve a fuss

House Of Parties started as a small collection of tablecloths and balloon garlands and grew into a full-service party rental studio for families across the Glendale area.

Our story

From one balloon arch to full-party styling

We started House Of Parties because every birthday shouldn't come with a last-minute scramble for plates, napkins and balloons. Today we rent and sell everything from themed tablecloths to full dessert-table setups, and we bring games and entertainment to keep the kids happy the whole time.

Every rental is inspected, cleaned and styled before it reaches your door — and every return is picked up on schedule so you never have to think twice.
